What's the Health Rhythms Study?

The Health Rhythms study is designed to monitor participants and establish a baseline for mental health. Therapy is not a complete answer, and its monitoring can be sporadic. Health Rhythms is a way to keep track of factors that impact mental health, and to create a new way to diagnose and aid people with depression, and other ailments

FAQS

How long does the study last?
Our study is designed to run for a total of 12 weeks. During this period, participants will be asked to regularly log their health metrics and participate in scheduled check-ins with our research team.
Is there any compensation for participating?
Yes, participants who complete the full 12-week program will receive a $100 gift card as compensation for their time and effort. Additional incentives may be available for those who meet all study requirements.
What kind of data will be collected?
We collect various health metrics including sleep patterns, physical activity levels, heart rate data, and self-reported wellness indicators. All data is securely stored and your privacy is our top priority.
Can I withdraw from the study once I've started?
Yes, participation is completely voluntary. You can withdraw from the study at any time without any negative consequences. We simply ask that you notify our research team if you decide to discontinue.
Are there any risks involved?
The study is observational and non-invasive, so there are minimal risks involved. You'll simply be tracking various aspects of your daily health habits and routines using our mobile application.
